How Does It Work?

Subscribers can appoint a Broker to manage their account. Brokers can create Scouts, Lots, and Negotiate on the subscribers' behalf. Throughout the process keep complete control and take no transactional risk by making use of a Broker. Only you submit the final Match and sign the final contract.

To appoint a Broker, follow these steps:

- Scout for Brokers

- Request to appoint

The commission fee of brokers is R3 per ton; only on a successful Match!
