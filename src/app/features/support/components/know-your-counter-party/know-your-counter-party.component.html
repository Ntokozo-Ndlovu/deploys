KYC-Party

Know your Counterparty ("KYC-party"), protect yourself

TAKE NOTE:
[Match Exchange is not a party to the Seller and Buyer agreement.
These are just guidelines for you to follow to better KYC-party.]

SELLER

The objective here is to ensure that you get paid in full, on time, for the product you deliver:

1. Is the Buyer (Off-Taker) a well-known company? If not, ask around, especially in your area.

2. Google the Buyer’s contact details, phone the company and ask to speak to the Procurement Manager. Is he aware of the transaction?

3. If you’ve agreed to terms, consider paying for a credit check. Does this company pay its bills?

BUYER

Your objective is to receive the quantity and quality product that you are paying for, and on time:

1. Is the Seller a known farmer in the area?

2. Phone procurement agents or farmers you know in the area and get a reference.

3. Are you aware of any problems with this farmer (producer)?

4. Phone the farmer and confirm the location from where the produce will be delivered.

5. Obtain information about the farmer’s other off-takers / customers or to who the farmer normally supplies to. Phone that off-taker and get a reference.

6. If you have agreed to any payments prior to delivery, consider a credit check or if possible an escrow account at an independent (and trusted) third party that may only release the payment on your receipt of the purchased goods.

We are not liable for any damages or losses whatsoever that may result for a transaction that was not successful.
